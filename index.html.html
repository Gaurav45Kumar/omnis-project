The Trillion Dollar Problem

When someone passes away, their financial and digital assets often remain inaccessible, creating significant challenges for families worldwide.

Cryptocurrency Assets

Without private keys, crypto wallets become permanently inaccessible, locking away billions in digital assets that traditional wills can't manage.

Bank Accounts & Insurance

Complex processes leave assets unclaimed. In India, over ₹78,000 Cr is in dormant bank deposits and ₹25,000 Cr in unclaimed insurance.

Stock Portfolios

Dematerialized shares and trading accounts often go unclaimed. Over ₹11,000 Crore in shares are currently held by the IEPF in India alone.

Digital Ghosts & Wills

Traditional wills are expensive and ignore digital assets. Social media, cloud accounts, and email vanish, leaving a "Digital Ghost" and locking away memories.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Omnis and digital inheritance.

How is Omnis different from a traditional will?

While a traditional will covers physical assets and is executed through probate court, Omnis specializes in digital and financial assets (like crypto, social media, and dormant accounts) that often fall outside traditional estate planning. We provide immediate, secure access without probate delays, and our AI discovers assets your heirs might not even know about. We are designed to work *with* your will, not replace it.

What happens if I'm temporarily unavailable?

Omnis has sophisticated inactivity monitoring with customizable thresholds. If you don't interact with your account for a predetermined period (which you set), we initiate a multi-step check-in process. Only after exhaustive verification (including attempts to contact you and your trusted contacts) of a permanent incapacity or passing will we begin the inheritance process.

How do you verify someone has passed away?

We use a multi-step verification process that combines technology and human oversight. This includes checking official death records, requiring certification from multiple designated trusted contacts, and our internal verification team. This human-centered approach prevents false triggers while ensuring legitimate cases are handled promptly and respectfully.

Is my data safe with Omnis?

Absolutely. We employ bank-level security with end-to-end encryption. We use a zero-knowledge architecture, so we *never* have access to your unencrypted data or your master password—not even for customer support. We conduct regular third-party security audits to ensure we maintain the highest protection standards and are compliant with global data protection regulations like GDPR.
